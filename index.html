Who We Are

Not just a production house—a transformation engine

Reel Beautiful isn't just a production house—it's a transformation engine. Founded by global power couple Inder Singh Randhawa (ISR) and Chandani, we operate at the elite intersection of high-stakes entertainment and high-end aesthetics.

Our founders have navigated the world's most prestigious stages, from the Grammy red carpet to #1 hits. The resume includes major labels like T-Series, Sony Music, Universal Music, Zee Music Co and Viacom18.

From films including National Award winning actor Rajkummar Rao, iconic Bo Derek to having a project like "Pind De Gerhe"—also known as "Kali Activa"—which is a cultural staple.

Now, stop worrying about the 'how' and start focusing on the 'what.' We provide the infrastructure and information for your success, turning your vision into a reality that leaves nothing to chance.

What We Do

Dual Excellence

The Reel World: Learn the Blueprint from Those Who've Done It.

Reel Beautiful is a global educational and informational sanctuary designed to bridge the gap between aspiration and mastery. We believe that while talent is innate, the roadmap to success is built on the shared wisdom of those who have walked the path before.

By deconstructing the personal journeys of artists—their pivots, their setbacks, and their breakthroughs—we provide the blueprint for the next generation of creators. Our mission is to transform listening, hearing, and reading into a powerful educational experience, fostering a community where the stories behind the art empower the art itself.

The Beauty World: Own your look, through knowledge.

With a journalism degree, a background in medical aesthetics, and years of front-line entertainment experience as the face of multiple major projects, Chandani brings an insider's lens to her platform, Reel Beautiful.

By deconstructing the secrets of the stars through interviews and conversations with top experts, Chandani provides the roadmap and education for her audience to gain the professional knowledge and lasting confidence needed to define their own signature style.
